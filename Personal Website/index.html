About

Meet Lucas, a 17-year-old high school student with a passion for web design and development. Graduating in June 2023, Lucas is eager to start freelancing and creating websites for clients. Along with a strong understanding of HTML, CSS, JS and JQuery, Lucas is also skilled in programming languages such as Python, C++, C#, and Java. In addition, Lucas has experience in game development using Unity and has a basic understanding of SQL and working with databases through Python and SQL. As Lucas looks to start a career as a software engineer, he is excited to bring his skills and creativity to the industry.
